Brownies

Description

Ghirardelli brownies have an amazing, chewy, chocolattely taste. Additionally, they are easy to make.

Recipe taken from Ghiradelli website.

Ingredients

  • 4 Ounces Ghirardelli Semi-sweet chocolate baking bar
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) Butter, unsalted, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup brown sugar, dark or light, packed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs, large
  • 3/4 cup + 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/8 teaspoon salt
